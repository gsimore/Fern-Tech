About

Where science meets software

Fern Tech bridges the gap between complex challenges and innovative technology solutions, combining deep software engineering expertise with environmental science knowledge.

With nearly a decade of experience building full-stack applications and cloud-based systems, Fern Tech has worked with companies ranging from early-stage startups to established organizations, delivering scalable software solutions across multiple industries. Technical expertise spans Python, Django, Vue.js, and modern cloud infrastructure, with a particular focus on AI integration and data systems.

A background in environmental science—including geochemistry, ecology, and water systems—provides a unique perspective on how technology can address sustainability challenges. This combination of rigorous scientific training and hands-on engineering experience enables solutions that are both technically robust and scientifically sound.

Working remotely with clients around the world, Fern Tech offers flexible consulting and development services tailored to each organization's needs.
